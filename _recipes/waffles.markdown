# About
Waffles. You can put butter, gravy, jelly, syrup, or chicken on them. You know what - put them all on at the same time.

# Ingrediants
•	2 egg
•	2c flour
•	1.75c milk
•	.5c oil
•	1T sugar
•	1T baking powder
•	.5t salt
•	.5t vanilla
•	1t cinnamon
•	1t baking soda

# Instructions
Mix everything together and place into a waffle maker.
